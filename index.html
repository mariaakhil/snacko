OUR ORIGIN

FROM FARM
TO TABLE.

Founded in 2018, Snacko began with a simple observation: the snack aisle lacked soul. We wanted to create something that honored the natural sweetness of tropical fruit while delivering the addictive crunch we all crave.

Our bananas are ethically sourced from family-owned farms and sliced within 24 hours of harvest to preserve their peak flavor. No artificial preservatives, no shortcuts—just pure, golden goodness.
