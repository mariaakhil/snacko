THE CULINARY ART

The Crunch Story

"Every chip tells a journey from the tropical groves to our kitchen. We don't just fry bananas; we preserve a moment of crisp perfection."

Our artisanal process begins with hand-selected green plantains, harvested at the peak of their starch-to-sugar ratio. We slice them ultra-thin before kettle-cooking them in small batches using pure, cold-pressed coconut oil.

This ancient technique ensures a snap that echoes with every bite, finished with a whisper of solar-evaporated sea salt.
